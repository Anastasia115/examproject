Description

TWISTER game challenges you to put your hands and feet at different places on the mat without falling over!

Be the last player standing to win. Two new moves!

With Spinner’s Choice, the spinner makes up a move for the other player to do.

Fun Spinner’s Choice ideas on the back of the spinner! If the spinner lands on Air, the player must put a hand or foot in the air!
